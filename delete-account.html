‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I temporarily deactivate my account instead?
A: Currently, we only offer permanent deletion. However, you can simply stop using the app and your data will remain saved until you decide to delete your account.
Q: Will I get a confirmation when my account is deleted?
A: Yes! If you delete via email, we'll send a confirmation email once the deletion is complete. In-app deletions show an immediate confirmation message.
Q: Can I download my data before deleting?
A: Yes! Before deleting, you can request a data export by emailing support@kamartec.online with subject "Data Export Request." We'll provide your data in a portable format within 7 days.
Q: Do you keep any data after deletion?
A: We may retain some anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you personally, and we may retain data as required by law for legal compliance purposes. All personally identifiable information is permanently deleted.
Q: Can I create a new account with the same email later?
A: Yes! After the deletion process is complete (typically 7 days), you can create a new FocusPal account using the same email address. However, none of your previous data will be restored.
Q: Why do you need 7 days to fully delete everything?
A: Our backup systems retain data temporarily for disaster recovery. This industry-standard practice ensures we can restore service if there's a technical failure. After 7 days, all backups are purged permanently.
Q: Is this compliant with GDPR and other privacy laws?
A: Yes! Our deletion process complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other major privacy regulations. You have the right to erasure, and we honor that right fully.
